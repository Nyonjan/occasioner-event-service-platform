Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At OCCASIONER, we collect personal information necessary to deliver exceptional event services. This includes:

  • Identity Information: Your name, phone number, and email address.
  • Location Data: The address and location details of the event or service site.
  • Booking Particulars: Specific requirements, dates, and times related to your hire.
  • Service Provider Details: Professional credentials, identity verification, and experience for all workers.

2. How We Use Information

We utilize the collected data to maintain the highest standards of our platform ecosystem:

  • To process and manage your event bookings and hire requests.
  • To securely connect customers with verified service professionals.
  • To improve the overall OCCASIONER platform experience and service quality.
  • To communicate essential booking updates, confirmations, and support.

3. Data Protection

The safety of your data is a primary concern. OCCASIONER implements the following safeguards:

  • Secure Storage: Data is stored using industry-standard encrypted storage solutions.
  • Restricted Access: Only authorized personnel have access for operational purposes.
  • Sharing Controls: No unauthorized sharing with external marketing entities.

4. Data Sharing

We believe in absolute transparency regarding your data. Information is only shared under these strict conditions:

  • Operational Necessity: Essential details are shared between the user and the assigned worker solely for booking fulfillment.
  • No Commercial Sale: OCCASIONER does NOT sell personal data to third parties.
  • Legal Compliance: Data may only be shared where required by law or for safety verification.

5. User Rights

As a user of our platform, you retain complete control over your information:

  • Users can request updates, corrections, or the complete deletion of their data.
  • Users can contact our support team at any time for data-related concerns or inquiries.

6. Cookies / Tracking

OCCASIONER uses basic tracking technologies and cookies to enhance the user experience, analyze site performance, and remember your session preferences. These do not extract personal files from your device.

7. Policy Updates

OCCASIONER reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect platform evolution or regulatory changes. We encourage users to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
