Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

1. For Users (Customers)

1.1 Booking Policy

Customers can browse and book various event services and curated occasion packages through the OCCASIONER platform. All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation from the assigned service professional.

1.2 Payment Rules

  • A 50% advance payment is required at the time of booking to secure the professional's services.
  • The remaining 50% balance must be settled within 12 hours of service completion.
  • Secure payments are processed through our integrated payment gateway.

1.3 Cancellation Policy

Users may cancel bookings through the dashboard. Late cancellations (made less than 24 hours before the event) may incur charges or forfeit the advance payment according to the professional's specific policy.

1.4 Platform Responsibility

OCCASIONER acts as a facilitator, connecting verified professionals with customers. While we ensure rigorous background checks and experience verification, the final service delivery is the responsibility of the assigned professional.

1.5 Worker Replacement Policy

If for any reason a customer is dissatisfied with an assigned professional, they may request a replacement professional through our support team at least 48 hours before the scheduled event.

2. For Service Providers (Workers)

2.1 Professionalism & Punctuality

All service providers must arrive at the scheduled location on time. Services must be completed professionally and to the standards defined by the platform's quality benchmarks.

2.2 Cancellation & Commitment

Last-minute cancellations without a valid and documented reason are strictly prohibited. Consistency and reliability are core requirements for maintaining an active profile on OCCASIONER.

2.3 Conduct & Behaviour

Service providers must maintain respectful behavior and absolute professionalism at all times. Professionals are expected to respect customer privacy and property.

2.4 Legal & Safety Compliance

Any illegal activities, including but not limited to theft, harassment, or unsafe practices, will result in immediate termination of the agreement and potential legal action.

2.5 System Integrity & Commission

  • Bypassing the OCCASIONER platform for direct deals with customers is strictly prohibited.
  • All service providers agree to the platform commission as stated in their individual agreements.
  • Violation of system integrity will result in permanent removal from the network.

2.6 Verification Clause

Professionals must provide a valid government-issued ID and documented proof of service experience. OCCASIONER reserves the right to approve or reject applications based on this verification.

3. Data Privacy

Your privacy is paramount to us at OCCASIONER. User and service provider data (including names, contact details, and addresses) are utilized exclusively for:

  • Facilitating bookings and service delivery.
  • Internal verification and background checks.
  • Essential communication regarding event details.

OCCASIONER does not sell, trade, or share personal data with unverified third parties for marketing purposes.
