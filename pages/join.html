Service Agreement

1. Worker Responsibilities

All service professionals are expected to arrive at scheduled locations promptly. Services must be rendered according to the quality benchmarks outlined in the professional orientation guide. Unauthorized cancellations or failure to attend confirmed bookings may lead to account suspension.

2. Safety & Professional Conduct

Unlawful activities, harassment, or disregard for safety protocols will result in immediate termination of the agreement. Professionals must maintain absolute discretion, respecting the privacy and property of every client.

3. Service Commitment

Professionals should only accept engagements for which they are fully available. Consistent fulfillment of accepted bookings is essential for maintaining a high-performance rating within the network.

4. Compensation & Platform Integrity

All financial transactions must be facilitated through the Occasioner platform. Bypassing the platform for direct payments is strictly prohibited and nullifies insurance coverage. Platform service fees are adjusted automatically at the point of disbursement.

5. Data Stewardship & Privacy

Personal data is utilized exclusively for identity verification, booking facilitation, and quality assurance. Occasioner adheres to strict data protection standards and does not share professional profiles with unverified external entities.

6. Verification & Onboarding

The onboarding process is subject to successful background checks and verification of professional credentials. Occasioner retains the absolute discretion to finalize or decline any partner application based on these findings.
